Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystals you will add specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number selected at the beginning of the game. You will lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal will be different. It is up to you to figure it out!

Each time a new game is started you will have a different winning number to get to. Each crystals value will also change.
